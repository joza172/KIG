About KFM Holding

KFM Holding is a global investment and business group connecting capital, projects, and partners worldwide. We bridge markets and opportunities, creating sustainable value through strategic partnerships and forward-thinking investment strategies.

Our firm grew from the Kaufman family's entrepreneurial tradition, combining deep-rooted values of trust and responsibility with a long-term vision. We merge heritage with modern investment strategies, building lasting relationships across international markets.

"KFM Holding grew from family tradition but with a forward-looking vision. Our mission is to build bridges between markets, connect capital and people, and leave a legacy for future generations."
